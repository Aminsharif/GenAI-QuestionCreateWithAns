Question:6. Why is ensuring access to clean drinking water and sanitation important, and what are the challenges in achieving this goal?
Question:Ensuring access to clean drinking water and sanitation is important because it is a basic human right and essential for health, well-being, and overall quality of life. Lack of access to clean water can lead to waterborne diseases, malnutrition, and even death. Sanitation is crucial for preventing the spread of diseases and maintaining hygiene.

Challenges in achieving this goal include water scarcity, especially in developing regions, where more than 40% of people are affected. Climate change is exacerbating this issue, leading to projected increases in water scarcity. Additionally, lack of infrastructure, pollution, and inadequate sanitation facilities are barriers to ensuring universal access to clean drinking water and sanitation.
